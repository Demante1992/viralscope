AI and OAuth Disclosure

AI Assistance

CommandCue uses AI to summarize metrics, suggest content strategy, identify possible growth opportunities, draft reports, and help creators plan next actions. AI output may be incomplete or incorrect and should be reviewed by the user.

OAuth Access

When you connect a provider, CommandCue requests only the scopes needed for the feature being used. YouTube analytics and Meta profile connections are handled through provider OAuth flows. Advanced Facebook/Instagram insights require additional Meta approval.

Human Control

CommandCue does not publish content, edit your channel, or message your audience without an explicit product feature and user action. Current AI features are advisory and workflow-focused.

Recommendations Are Not Guarantees

Growth, revenue, sponsor outcomes, audience quality, and platform performance can vary widely. CommandCue recommendations should be treated as strategy support, not guaranteed results.
