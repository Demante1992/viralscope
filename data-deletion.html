Data Deletion

CommandCue users can request account, workspace, OAuth token, tracked URL, and analytics snapshot deletion.

How To Request Deletion

  1. Log into your CommandCue account.
  2. Open Settings, then Data Privacy.
  3. Choose Request account deletion or revoke connected provider tokens.
  4. If you cannot log in, contact support from the email associated with the account.

What Gets Deleted

Deletion covers account profile data, workspace data, connected OAuth tokens, tracked URLs, saved reports, and analytics snapshots held by CommandCue. Some billing records may remain in Stripe for tax, fraud, compliance, and payment-history requirements.

Connected Platforms

Deleting CommandCue data does not delete your YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Twitch, Kick, or website data from those services. You may also revoke CommandCue access inside each platform's connected apps/settings area.
