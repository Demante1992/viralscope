Privacy Policy

This starter policy is for beta launch preparation and should be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before paid public launch.

What CommandCue Collects

CommandCue collects account information such as name, email, plan status, connected platform labels, tracked URLs, and workspace settings. If you connect platforms such as YouTube or Meta, CommandCue may store encrypted access tokens and profile or content metadata returned by those platforms.

How Data Is Used

We use data to operate dashboards, sync creator metrics, run AI growth recommendations, manage billing, improve product reliability, and help users understand traffic and revenue performance.

OAuth and Connected Platforms

Connected platform data is used only to power CommandCue features requested by the user. You can disconnect providers in settings. Advanced Meta/Facebook/Instagram metrics require additional permissions and app review before they can sync.

Payments

Payments are handled through Stripe. CommandCue does not store full card numbers. Billing identifiers, subscription state, and checkout status may be stored to manage plan access.

AI Processing

AI features may process channel summaries, metrics, posts, links, and user-entered prompts to generate recommendations. Do not submit confidential third-party data unless you have permission.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain account and workspace data while your account is active. You can request export or deletion through the data deletion page.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact the CommandCue owner using the support email that will be added before public launch.
